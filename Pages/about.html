Who Am I?

My name is Raffaele Esposito, and I am from Naples. I am credited with creating the first pizza pie.

Why did I make this site??

I wanted a place where people could share their opinions on pizza and learn more about this fascinating dish. I also wanted to show off my creation and spread the word about it.

What is Pizza?

According to Merriam-Webster Dictionary, pizza is "a dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked."
